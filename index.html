What is SPUD?

SPUD is an innovative gaming controller designed to revolutionize accessibility in gaming. Our mission is to create an affordable, compact, and versatile controller that enables one-handed gameplay without compromising on functionality or performance.

Developed as a capstone project at the University of Colorado Boulder by Adam Millman and Kai Franks, SPUD combines ergonomic design with cutting-edge technology to create a gaming controller that's both accessible and intuitive. Our controller features customizable button layouts, motion controls, and seamless integration with existing gaming platforms.

Why Make SPUD?

Money

Accessiblity controllers are 2-3 times the price of a traditional gaming controller. In order to combat this a make sure SPUD was accessible to everyone, we tried our best to develop the controller around the price of traditional gaming controller. We achieved this through the utulization of 3D printers and Arduinos.

Size

Accessiblity controllers take up a lot of space. SPUD brings back the familiarity and compactness of traditional gaming controllers into hands

User-Centered Design

Every aspect of SPUD was designed with the end user in mind, ensuring intuitive interfaces and seamless experiences across all touchpoints.

Familiarity

Basing Spud around controllers that already exist we were able to create a controller that has that familiar feel but also pack a punch.
