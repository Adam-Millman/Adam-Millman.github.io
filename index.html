Why Make SPUD?

Money

Accessiblity controllers are 2-3 times the price of a traditional gaming controller. In order to combat this a make sure SPUD was accessible to everyone, we tried our best to develop the controller around the price of traditional gaming controller. We achieved this through the utulization of 3D printers and Arduinos.

Size

Accessiblity controllers take up a lot of space. SPUD brings back the familiarity and compactness of traditional gaming controllers into hands

User-Centered Design

Every aspect of SPUD was designed with the end user in mind, ensuring intuitive interfaces and seamless experiences across all touchpoints.

Sustainability

We've built SPUD with long-term viability in mind, incorporating adaptable architecture that can evolve with changing technologies and user needs.
