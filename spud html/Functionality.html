Controller Functionality

Digital Prototyping

The SPUD controller was initially designed as a two part system. The first part was a handheld controller that was used to control the second part, a wearable controller. each controlling the major functionality and the movement around a 3D space respectively.

The handheld controller integrates four buttons, a joystick, and an accelerometer. Doing most of the heavy lifting when it comes to the functionality of the system.

The wearable controller features only a microcontroller and an accelerometer to control teh players aim in a 3D environtment.
